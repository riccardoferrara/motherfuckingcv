Presentation

Following a fulfilling academic journey that included earning a PhD, completing postdoctoral work, and teaching at a university level, I embarked on a new adventure by creating a patented software that would eventually serve as the foundation for my start-up, Sim&Cure. In my role at Sim&Cure, I had the opportunity to experience a diverse range of roles, from spearheading R&D projects (utilizing Python and C++) to leading quality management protocols across various departments, including risk management, clinical validation, training, production, and customer support. Thanks to our team's tireless efforts, we achieved CE marking for our medical devices, as well as ISO13485 and FDA510(k) certifications.

One of my key contributions to Sim&Cure was designing our platform on Salesforce, which allowed us to link all our activities through a centralized database. I oversaw the implementation of this platform, which facilitated customer support ticketing, R&D ticketing, non-conformity management, product releases, versioning, pay-per-use usage tokens, licensing, invoicing, internal and external certification training, and clinical validation. During my time at Sim&Cure, I also played a vital role in scaling the company during two fundraising rounds and launching our satellite office in the United States, where I worked closely with customers to evangelize our technology.

Following my tenure at Sim&Cure, I designed and developed several exciting projects for the Italian railway network, including interactive maps (using Node, Python, and HTML), a purchasing request management platform (utilizing SQL), and weather alert systems (using Node and Python). Additionally, I created an e-commerce platform for another client using WordPress, JavaScript, and HTML. Recently, I had the pleasure of teaching at Tortuga Coders, a highly regarded web development bootcamp, where I taught courses on Node, SQL, React, and Express.

Currently, I hold the position of Lead Developer at Foundever (formerly known as Sitel Group), one of the world's largest outsourcing companies. In this role, I continue to utilize my extensive skills in software design, development, and implementation, while also taking on new challenges and opportunities for growth.
